# AERO-MR User Manual

## 1. Getting Started
1. Put on the HoloLens 2 and launch the AERO-MR app.
2. Select your training mission from the virtual menu.
3. Ensure your RC Transmitter is connected and calibrated.

## 2. Flight Controls
- **Left Stick (Vertical)**: Throttle (Lift).
- **Right Stick (Vertical)**: Pitch (Forward/Backward).
- **Right Stick (Horizontal)**: Roll (Side-to-side).
- **Left Stick (Horizontal)**: Yaw (Rotation).

## 3. Training Scenarios
- **Takeoff**: Smoothly increase throttle until the drone hovers at 1.5m.
- **Waypoints**: Follow the green virtual rings through the environment.
- **Landing**: Land precisely on the virtual platform.

## 4. Using the Dashboard
1. Open the Dashboard link (provided by your Admin).
2. View your "Stability Score" trend.
3. Check "Collision Counts" to identify room for improvement.
4. Export session data to CSV for external analysis.
